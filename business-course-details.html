Business English

Enhance Your Professional Communication Skills

Objectives

  • Our Business English course is tailored for professionals who want to:
  • Communicate confidently and effectively in business environments.
  • Understand and use industry-specific vocabulary and jargon.
  • Develop skills for writing professional emails, reports, and proposals.
  • Master negotiation, presentation, and networking skills.

Eligibility

  • This course is ideal for professionals, managers, and business owners who:
  • Possess a basic to intermediate understanding of English.
  • Are looking to overcome communication barriers in the workplace.
  • Aim to enhance their ability to interact with international clients and teams.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to Business English

  • Corporate Communication

    Techniques for effective meetings, calls, and workplace discussions.

  • Professional Writing Skills

    Crafting concise and impactful reports, proposals, and presentations.

  • Negotiation and Persuasion

    Strategies to express ideas confidently and negotiate successfully.

  • Industry-Specific Language
